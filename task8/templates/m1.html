Toddlers' Temperature Range

Normal Temperature Range

The normal body temperature for toddlers (ages 1-3 years) typically falls between:

36.1°C to 37.2°C

When to Be Concerned

If a toddler's temperature deviates from the normal range, consider the following:

  • Mild Fever: 37.5°C to 38.0°C
  • Fever: Above 38.0°C
  • Hypothermia: Below 35.0°C

Measurement Methods

Common methods for measuring temperature in toddlers include:

  • Oral
  • Axillary (underarm)
  • Tympanic (ear)

Rectal measurements are more accurate, especially for younger children, but they are less common for toddlers.

Acceptable Temperature Range: 16°C to 20°C (60°F to 68°F)
Reason: Toddlers have better body temperature regulation than younger infants, but maintaining a stable temperature still contributes to their comfort and sleep quality.

Always seek medical advice if your toddler's temperature is outside the normal range.
