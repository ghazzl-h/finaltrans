Normal Temperature Ranges for Infants

The typical temperature range for infants is:

36.5°C to 37.5°C

Mild fever: 37.6°C to 38°C

Fever: Above 38°C

Hypothermia: Below 36°C

Temperature may vary slightly depending on the time of day, activity, or measurement method. Rectal measurements are the most accurate for infants. Always consult a healthcare professional for guidance if necessary.
