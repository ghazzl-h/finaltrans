Normal Temperature Ranges for Newborns

The typical temperature range for newborns is:

36.5°C to 37.4°C

Mild fever: 37.5°C to 37.9°C

Fever: Above 38°C

Hypothermia: Below 36°C

Rectal measurements are the most accurate for newborns. If the temperature falls outside the normal range, consult a healthcare provider immediately.

Acceptable Temperature Range: 16°C to 20°C (60°F to 68°F)
Reason: Infants in this age group are not yet able to regulate their body temperature well, so it’s critical to maintain a consistent room temperature within this range to prevent overheating or cooling.
